Delete Your Data

TCG Manager  ·  Data Deletion Request

You can request deletion of your TCG Manager account and all associated data at any time. We will permanently delete the following within 30 days of your request:

  • Your account (email and username)
  • Your card collection
  • Your decks
  • Your match history
  • Your wishlist

Option 1 — Delete from within the app

1
Open TCG Manager and sign in to your account.
2
Tap Profile (bottom navigation bar).
3
Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
4
Confirm the deletion. Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

Option 2 — Email us

If you are unable to access the app, email us from the address associated with your account:

sudo@jeffisit.tech
Subject: Data Deletion Request

Please include your registered email address in the message. We will confirm deletion within 30 days.
