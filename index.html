Privacy Policy

TCG Manager  ·  Last updated: March 25, 2026

Your data belongs to you. TCG Manager does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with advertisers or third parties for marketing purposes.

1. Who We Are

TCG Manager is a trading card game collection manager and deck tracker for Magic: The Gathering, Pokémon, and Disney Lorcana. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the app's features:

  • Account information – email address and username when you register.
  • Collection data – cards you add to your collection, including quantities, conditions, and locations.
  • Deck data – decks you create and the cards within them.
  • Match history – match results you log, including opponent names and notes you choose to enter.
  • Wishlist – cards you add to your wishlist (Ultimate tier).
  • Subscription status – your active subscription tier, managed via RevenueCat.

We do not collect location data, contacts, browsing history, or any data beyond what you explicitly enter into the app.

3. Camera Permission

TCG Manager requests camera access solely to scan trading cards for quick collection entry. Camera images are processed on-device using Google ML Kit and are never uploaded to our servers or stored.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and sync your collection, decks, match history, and wishlist across devices.
  • To display your collection statistics and match win rates.
  • To manage your subscription and unlock premium features.
  • To authenticate your account securely.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

5. Third-Party Services

TCG Manager uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase – Our backend database and authentication provider. Your account data, collection, decks, and match history are stored on Supabase's servers. Supabase Privacy Policy →
  • RevenueCat – Manages in-app subscriptions and purchase verification. RevenueCat receives a pseudonymous user ID and your subscription status. RevenueCat Privacy Policy →
  • Google ML Kit – Used on-device for card text recognition and barcode scanning. No data leaves your device. Google ML Kit Terms →
  • Scryfall / TCGdex / Lorcana API – Public card data APIs used to search and display card information. We send card search queries; no personal data is transmitted.

The Shop feature links to TCGPlayer, Amazon, and eBay using affiliate links. Tapping these links opens your device's browser; those sites have their own privacy policies and we do not share any personal data with them.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data (collection, decks, match history, wishlist) is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

7. Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored in Supabase with row-level security policies, meaning each user can only access their own data. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

8. Children's Privacy

TCG Manager is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access – request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Delete – request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Correct – update inaccurate information via the app's profile settings.
  • Export – the app provides CSV export of your collection data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes via an in-app notice. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision.
